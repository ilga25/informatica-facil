What is Email?

Introduction

Email is one of the most important tools on the Internet. It allows us to send messages and files quickly worldwide.

What is email?

Email is a digital messaging system that allows people to communicate through the Internet.

What is it used for?

  • Send messages
  • Attach files
  • Create online accounts
  • Professional communication

Parts of an email

  • Sender
  • Recipient
  • Subject
  • Message body
  • Attachments

Email providers

  • Gmail
  • Outlook
  • Yahoo Mail

Safe usage tips

  • Do not open suspicious emails
  • Never share passwords
  • Use strong passwords

Common scams

Beware of emails asking for personal or banking information.

Conclusion

Mastering email is essential for work, study and daily life.
