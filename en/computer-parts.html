Introduction

A computer is made up of different physical parts that work together to process information. Knowing these parts will help you understand how your device works and how to take better care of it.

Hardware and software

Hardware refers to the physical parts: screen, keyboard, mouse, etc. Software refers to programs. Both are needed for a computer to work properly.

External devices

  • Screen or monitor
  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
  • Speakers
  • Printer

The case or CPU

Inside the case are the most important components of the computer.

Processor

It is the brain of the computer. It executes instructions and controls all operations.

RAM memory

It stores temporary information while the computer is turned on. The more RAM you have, the faster your computer will work.

Hard drive

It is where files, programs and the operating system are stored.

Graphics card

It processes the images you see on the screen.

Motherboard

It is the main board where all components are connected.

Power supply

It provides electrical power to the computer.

Why knowing the parts is important

Knowing the parts of a computer allows you to:

  • Detect problems
  • Improve performance
  • Choose wisely when buying a computer

Conclusion

A computer is a complex but understandable machine. Knowing its parts makes you a more aware and prepared user.
