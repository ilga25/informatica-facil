Introduction

Computer security is a fundamental aspect of using a computer and the Internet. It protects your personal data, your files and your privacy against increasingly frequent threats.

What is computer security?

Computer security consists of a set of measures aimed at protecting computer systems, networks and data from unauthorized access, theft, damage or manipulation.

Main threats

  • Viruses and malware
  • Email scams
  • Password theft
  • Identity theft
  • Spyware

What is a computer virus?

A computer virus is a malicious program designed to damage or control your computer without your permission. It can delete files, slow down the system or steal information.

How do viruses get in?

  • Downloaded files
  • Fake emails
  • Unsafe websites
  • Infected USB drives

How to protect your computer

  • Install an up-to-date antivirus
  • Do not download files from suspicious sites
  • Do not open suspicious emails
  • Update the operating system
  • Make regular backups

The importance of passwords

A secure password must be hard to guess. It should include letters, numbers and symbols, and should not be reused across many accounts.

Tips for creating secure passwords

  • Do not use dates or names
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase and numbers
  • Do not write them in visible places
  • Change them regularly

Most common scams

Some of the most common scams include:

  • Fake bank emails
  • Non-existent prizes
  • Offers that are too good to be true
  • Fake urgent messages

How to browse safely

  • Check that websites are secure
  • Do not click on suspicious ads
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive operations

Importance of backups

A backup allows you to recover your data if something goes wrong. It is recommended to make them periodically on an external drive or in the cloud.

Security and responsibility

The best protection is the combination of suitable programs and a responsible user. Most problems can be avoided by acting with common sense.

Conclusion

Computer security is not only for experts. Any user can protect themselves by following basic good practices. Taking care of your information is as important as taking care of your home or your money.
